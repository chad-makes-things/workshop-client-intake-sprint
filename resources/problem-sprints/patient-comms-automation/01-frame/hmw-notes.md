# How Might We (HMW) Notes

## Theme 1: Lead Response Speed & Reliability
1. **HMW** ensure every inbound lead (call or form) gets a response within 60 minutes, even when the front desk is busy with patients?
2. **HMW** create a seamless handoff between AI phone agents and human staff so the patient never feels "passed around"?
3. **HMW** prioritize leads by estimated value so high-intent inquiries (Invisalign, implants) get fastest response?
4. **HMW** make form fill monitoring automatic instead of depending on someone checking an inbox?

## Theme 2: Treatment Follow-Up Automation
5. **HMW** automate treatment follow-up so it happens consistently without requiring TC time or emotional labor?
6. **HMW** make follow-up messages feel personal and caring rather than "salesy" — even when automated?
7. **HMW** use the 3-day / 7-day / 14-day / 30-day cadence that closes 50%+ more treatment without being annoying?
8. **HMW** handle the $20k+ cases that need 4-5 touchpoints differently from routine treatment follow-ups?
9. **HMW** let TCs review and personalize automated messages so they feel ownership without bearing the execution burden?

## Theme 3: Data Connectivity & Triggers
10. **HMW** extract appointment, treatment plan, and patient data from 75+ different PMS systems to trigger automated communications?
11. **HMW** connect AI note-taker output (in-room treatment mentions) to follow-up triggers even when a formal treatment plan wasn't built?
12. **HMW** build a minimum viable trigger set that works across all PMS systems without needing deep integrations with each one?

## Theme 4: Rebooking & Retention
13. **HMW** ensure patients never leave the office without their next appointment — and catch the ones who do within 24 hours?
14. **HMW** create an automated rebooking sequence that's friendly and persistent without being pushy?
15. **HMW** track rebooking conversion rates per practice so HQ can identify and intervene on underperformers?

## Theme 5: Database Reactivation
16. **HMW** systematically re-engage lapsed patients (12+ months) with a friendly, low-pressure outreach cadence?
17. **HMW** segment the lapsed database by last treatment type to personalize reactivation messages?
18. **HMW** measure reactivation ROI so practices can see the revenue recovered from "dead" leads?

## Theme 6: Playbook Standardization vs. Customization
19. **HMW** create a base playbook that works for 90%+ of practices while allowing practice-type variants (cosmetic vs. family vs. specialty)?
20. **HMW** make the playbook self-improving — learning from which messages get responses across the network?
21. **HMW** deploy the playbook without requiring practices to adopt a new dashboard or CRM interface?

## Theme 7: Staff Adoption & Culture
22. **HMW** frame automated follow-up as "patient care" rather than "sales" to overcome TC resistance?
23. **HMW** give office managers visibility into what the automation is doing without requiring them to manage it?
24. **HMW** make the 20-minute weekly human touchpoint feel valuable and easy rather than like another task?

## Theme 8: Compliance & Trust
25. **HMW** ensure all automated patient communications are HIPAA-compliant and properly consented?
