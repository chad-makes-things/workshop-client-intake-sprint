# Research & Inspiration — Practice Media Strategy

## What We Already Know Works

### Dr. Grant Olson / Innovative Dental (Internal Proof)
- 138K YouTube subscribers, 23M+ views
- Content pyramid: 1 pillar video → 14-15 platform-specific pieces
- Production schedule: Record Tuesday, edit Wednesday, distribute Thursday, engage Friday
- The "steak moment" — signature content format that proves implant outcomes with sensory proof
- **Why it works**: Dedicated production team, consistent cadence, feedback loop (views → patients → more stories)
- **Why it doesn't scale**: Dr. Olson's setup requires a professional production team. 260 practices can't each hire a video editor.

### The Content Repurposing Matrix (Internal Strategy)
- Already designed: 156 monthly pieces from 11 pillar inputs
- 1:14 multiplication factor per pillar piece
- **Problem**: The matrix is starving. No raw content flowing in from practices.

### The Hub-and-Spoke Social Model (Internal Strategy)
- Central brand pages create high-production assets
- Local pages receive monthly "content kits" to customize and post
- **Problem**: Local pages have nothing authentic to post. Content kits are generic.

## Existing Market Solutions (Gaps Analysis)

### Category 1: Dental Marketing Platforms
**Players**: Birdeye, Podium, Weave, RevenueWell, NexHealth
- **What they do**: Review management, patient communication, appointment reminders, basic social posting
- **Gap**: None of them solve content capture. They can post to social media, but they don't help practices CREATE the content. No PMS-triggered capture prompts. No production pipeline.

### Category 2: Multi-Location Social Management
**Players**: Sprout Social, Hootsuite, SOCi, Reputation.com
- **What they do**: Centralized social media management, content calendars, approval workflows, reporting
- **Gap**: Built for distributing content, not capturing it. Assume content already exists. No mobile capture workflow. No AI processing pipeline.

### Category 3: Employee/User-Generated Content Platforms
**Players**: Greenfly, TINT, Miappi, Photobooth Supply Co
- **What they do**: Request and collect content from employees/advocates, organize UGC, rights management
- **Closest to our need**: Greenfly specifically does "content assignments" — push a request to an employee, they capture on phone, it flows back to HQ
- **Gap**: Not healthcare-specific. No PMS integration. No dental-specific playbooks. No automated production pipeline (color correction, formatting, captioning). No HIPAA considerations.

### Category 4: AI Video Editing / Repurposing
**Players**: Opus Clip, Vidyo.ai, Descript, Kapwing, Pictory
- **What they do**: Take long-form video, auto-detect highlights, cut into shorts/reels with captions
- **Relevant capability**: Could be a component of the production pipeline
- **Gap**: Standalone tools. Don't connect to capture workflow. Don't know dental context.

### Category 5: Content Capture Hardware
**Players**: Insta360, GoPro, Ray-Ban Meta, Mevo, phone tripod systems
- **What they do**: Simplified capture hardware for various contexts
- **Relevant**: Insta360 in operatory (passive capture), Ray-Ban Meta (hands-free POV), tripod+phone (testimonials)
- **Gap**: Hardware alone doesn't solve the behavior problem. Need the prompt + upload + pipeline.

---

## Key Insight: Nothing Exists That Does All Three

The market has:
1. Tools that **distribute** content (Sprout, Hootsuite, SOCi)
2. Tools that **collect** content from people (Greenfly, TINT)
3. Tools that **process** content with AI (Opus Clip, Descript)

**Nobody combines**: PMS-triggered alerts → guided mobile capture → AI processing → automated multi-channel distribution — specifically for healthcare/dental at scale.

This is the gap. The solution needs to be a vertically integrated system, not a stitched-together stack of generic tools.

## Design Inspiration

### Uber Driver App Pattern
- Shows driver what to do next
- One-tap actions
- Real-time feedback on performance
- Gamification (rating, earnings visibility)
- **Apply to**: The Content Champion app. Shows today's content opportunities. One-tap to start capture. See your content go live. Scorecard.

### Duolingo Habit Pattern
- Daily streak/cadence
- Push notification at the right time
- Micro-tasks (2-3 minutes, not 30)
- Celebratory feedback when completed
- **Apply to**: Building the daily content capture habit. "You're on a 14-day capture streak!"

### Instagram Stories Capture Pattern
- Open camera → capture → basic edit → post
- Entire flow under 30 seconds
- No complexity, no file management
- **Apply to**: The actual capture UX. Open app → camera is ready → take photo/video → confirm → done.
