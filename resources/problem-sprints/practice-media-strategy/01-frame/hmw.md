# How Might We Notes — Practice-Level Media Strategy

## Capture & Trigger

1. **HMW** connect the PMS appointment schedule to a content capture alert so the champion knows BEFORE the patient arrives that this is a capture opportunity?

2. **HMW** make content capture feel like checking a box rather than doing a project? (Under 60 seconds, phone only)

3. **HMW** remind the champion about post-op content at exactly the right moment — 2 weeks after surgery when the result is visible and the patient is thrilled?

4. **HMW** capture content passively (Insta360 cameras, Ray-Ban Meta glasses, always-on room cameras) so staff don't have to remember anything?

5. **HMW** build the before/after capture into the clinical workflow itself (part of the chart, not an afterthought)?

---

## Behavior & Habit

6. **HMW** turn content capture into a game — scorecard, leaderboard, recognition — so practices compete to produce the best content?

7. **HMW** show the champion the finished product (their raw capture → polished Instagram post) within 24-48 hours so they see the payoff?

8. **HMW** make the doctor's involvement so minimal (hold phone, say 4 minutes of words, done) that they never say no?

9. **HMW** create a "content champion" role that staff actually want — with title, recognition, maybe certification?

10. **HMW** use the daily huddle (already happening at most practices) as the trigger point for content planning?

---

## Production & Automation

11. **HMW** automatically process raw captures — color correction, cropping, branding — without any human editing?

12. **HMW** take a single 4-minute doctor video and automatically cut it into a 45-second Short, a 15-second Reel, and a quote graphic?

13. **HMW** use AI to generate captions, hashtags, and descriptions from raw video so HQ doesn't bottleneck on copywriting?

14. **HMW** maintain brand consistency across 260 practice storefronts while still feeling locally authentic?

15. **HMW** build a content queue that HQ can review/approve in bulk rather than piece by piece?

---

## Distribution & Routing

16. **HMW** automatically route a before/after from a Springfield implant case to Instagram, the practice website, AND Dr. Olson's YouTube content pipeline?

17. **HMW** decide which content goes to which channel without a human making that decision every time? (Rules engine by content type + practice type)

18. **HMW** feed the digital storefront (from prior sprint) with fresh content automatically so practice websites don't go stale?

19. **HMW** surface the best-performing practice content to the SGA brand level for network-wide distribution?

20. **HMW** integrate webinar/live content capture for practices doing recurring live YouTube sessions?

---

## Scale & Infrastructure

21. **HMW** define a hardware kit per practice type (Playbook 1-4) that's pre-configured and ships in a box?

22. **HMW** onboard a new acquisition's staff to the content system in under 2 hours?

23. **HMW** handle patient consent at intake (photo/video release) so it's never a blocker at the moment of capture?

24. **HMW** scale from the initial 10-15 engaged practices to all 260+ without the system breaking?

25. **HMW** measure content's impact on actual patient bookings, not just vanity metrics (views/likes)?

---

## Patient Journey

26. **HMW** identify high-value cases coming up (implant, full-arch, cosmetic) from the PMS data and flag them as "content opportunities" days in advance?

27. **HMW** use the virtual consultation photos (already being submitted for implant cases) as the "before" so the capture burden is even lower?

28. **HMW** create a patient journey that naturally produces content — from initial photo submission through treatment through the 2-week reveal?

29. **HMW** turn the post-treatment follow-up (already scheduled) into the "after" capture moment without adding a separate step?

30. **HMW** make patients WANT to participate — featured on YouTube, part of a transformation story — so they're asking to be captured?
