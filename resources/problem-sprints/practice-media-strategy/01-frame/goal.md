# Sprint Goal — Practice-Level + SGA Brand Media Strategy

## The 30-Day Goal

**In 30 days, can we design a system that turns every SGA practice AND the SGA brand itself into content-producing machines — alerting the right people to capture opportunities, simplifying capture to a single phone interaction, and automating all downstream production and distribution — so that HQ stops depending on broken human behavior and starts getting a steady stream of before/afters, testimonials, thought leadership, and brand content from 260+ locations and the leadership team?**

---

## Why This Matters

SGA Dental Partners is targeting **15% growth** across 260+ locations. Industry norm is 0-2%. They're currently at ~4%. The #1 barrier to hitting 15% is **operational — broken human behavior at the practice level.**

Specifically:
- Practices **never provide before/afters** despite being asked repeatedly
- There is no system to identify upcoming high-value cases and prompt content capture
- Current photo/video upload tools are "shit" (direct quote)
- There is no downstream pipeline — even when content IS captured, it sits unused
- Dr. Grant Olson's Innovative Dental (138K YouTube subscribers, 23M+ views) proves the content model works — but it hasn't been replicated to other practices because the capture-to-publish pipeline doesn't exist at scale

## The Content Gap

| What Exists | What's Missing |
|------------|----------------|
| Dr. Olson's 138K-subscriber YouTube channel proving video drives patients | A system to replicate content capture at 260 other locations |
| A content repurposing matrix that produces 156 monthly pieces from 11 pillars | Raw content from practices to feed the matrix |
| A social growth strategy across 5 platforms | Authentic, practice-level content to post |
| A digital storefront system (from prior sprint) | Fresh media to populate those storefronts |
| Hub-and-spoke social model designed | Spokes (practices) producing nothing to distribute |

## The Human Behavior Problem

The biggest issue is **not technology** — it's that practice staff don't capture content because:
1. **No trigger** — Nobody tells them "this patient tomorrow is a great before/after case"
2. **No simple tool** — Current upload workflows are clunky and friction-heavy
3. **No feedback loop** — Staff never see their captures turn into polished content
4. **No habit** — There's no daily/weekly cadence or accountability system

## Scale Context

| Segment | Count | Content Priority |
|---------|-------|-----------------|
| Specialty (referral-based) | ~40 locations | Before/afters critical for referral marketing |
| Cosmetic/Implant (high-value) | ~25+ locations | Highest ROI for video content — implant transformations, full-arch reveals |
| General Practice | ~160 locations | Basic before/afters, testimonials, office culture |
| **Total** | **260+ locations** | **System must scale across all, prioritize high-value first** |

## Success Criteria

1. A practice social media champion can capture a before/after in under 60 seconds
2. The system proactively alerts staff about upcoming capture opportunities
3. Captured content is automatically processed (color correction, formatting) and routed to the right channels
4. HQ has real-time visibility into which practices are producing content and which aren't
5. Content flows to the digital storefront, social channels, and paid media pipeline
6. The system supports 4 practice playbooks + 1 SGA brand playbook
7. Hardware infrastructure (tripods, cameras, phones) is defined per practice type
8. SGA leadership (Jensen, Ellingson, Wong, Olson) receives weekly topic prompts and can capture brand content with the same simple mobile workflow
9. Partner stories, event content, and network milestones are captured through the same infrastructure and routed to B2B channels (LinkedIn, recruitment, acquisition pipeline)
