### X = Current Break Points

Every `X` on this map represents a place where the current system fails:

1. **Case → Trigger** (X): The PMS knows a full-arch case is coming Thursday. Nobody connects that data to a content capture prompt.

2. **Trigger → Capture** (X): Even when staff know they should capture, the tools are clunky. Photo upload workflows are friction-heavy. Video feels like too much effort. No guidance on what to capture.

3. **Capture → Production** (X): When content IS captured (rarely), it goes into a shared folder or gets texted to someone. There's no automated ingestion, quality check, color correction, or formatting pipeline.

4. **Production → Distribution** (X): Edited content doesn't have routing logic. Which channel does this before/after go to? The practice's Instagram? The SGA corporate YouTube? The digital storefront? Nobody decides, so nothing moves.

5. **Distribution → Impact** (X): Without a steady stream of practice-level content, the social channels post generic corporate content. Patients don't engage. Growth stays at 4%.

## Key Actors

| Actor | Role | Current State | Desired State |
|-------|------|--------------|---------------|
| **Social Media Champion** (dental assistant, typically) | Content capture at practice | Does nothing — no system, no habit, no tool | Gets daily alerts on their phone. Captures in <60 seconds. Sees their content go live. |
| **Doctor** | Clinical talent, face of content | Too busy to remember content, resistant to "extra work" | Holds phone for 4 minutes when prompted. Records a topic. Gets out of the way. |
| **HQ Marketing Team** (Dakota's team) | Content production and distribution | Begs practices for content. Gets nothing. Creates generic filler. | Receives steady stream of raw content. Processes through automated pipeline. Publishes to right channels. |
| **Practice Manager** | Oversees practice operations | Not involved in content strategy | Sees content scorecard. Knows their practice's content health. |
| **Patient** | The subject (and the beneficiary) | Has no idea their case could be featured | Consents at intake. Gets tagged for follow-up content capture at post-op visits. |

## The 4 Practice Playbooks

Different practice types need different content strategies:

### Playbook 1: Implant/Cosmetic Practices (~25+ locations)
- **Content focus**: Full-arch reveals, before/afters, cost transparency, patient journeys
- **Capture frequency**: Daily (high case volume, high-value content)
- **Hardware**: Tripod, ring light, Insta360 camera in operatory, phone mount
- **Dr. involvement**: High — face of content, 4-min topic recordings weekly

### Playbook 2: Specialty/Referral Practices (~40 locations)
- **Content focus**: Before/afters for referring GPs, case studies, surgical outcomes
- **Capture frequency**: 2-3x/week (case-driven)
- **Hardware**: Phone mount, basic tripod
- **Dr. involvement**: Medium — before/after capture, occasional case walkthrough

### Playbook 3: High-Performing GPs (growth-focused subset of ~160)
- **Content focus**: Smile makeovers, family testimonials, office culture, community involvement
- **Capture frequency**: 2-3x/week
- **Hardware**: Phone mount, basic tripod
- **Dr. involvement**: Low-Medium — testimonial prompts, culture content

### Playbook 4: Standard GPs (~remaining locations)
- **Content focus**: Google review captures, basic before/afters, team photos
- **Capture frequency**: Weekly
- **Hardware**: Phone only
- **Dr. involvement**: Low — review prompts, team photos

| System | Integration | Purpose |
|--------|------------|---------|
| **Practice Management System** | Read appointment schedule, case type, patient history | Identify capture opportunities and trigger alerts |
| **Digital Storefront** (prior sprint) | Push finished content to practice websites | Keep storefronts fresh with authentic content |
| **Social Media Channels** | Route content to correct practice/brand channels | Automated publishing per playbook |
| **Paid Media Pipeline** | Surface high-performing organic content for paid amplification | Dr. Olson content repurposing at scale |
| **Patient Consent System** | Capture photo/video release at intake | Legal compliance for content usage |
| **CRM/Patient Journey** | Trigger post-op capture reminders (2 weeks after surgery) | Before/after completion, testimonial timing |
