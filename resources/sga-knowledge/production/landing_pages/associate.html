What Early-Career Dentists Ask Us

And the real answers.

Your current DSO hired you to produce. SGA pairs you with a mentor through the Individual Growth Engine, gives you Pikos Institute access for world-class implant training, offers Class A shareholder equity from day one, and measures your success by clinical growth -- not just collections. No corporate DSO offers an integrated education platform, 1:1 mentorship, and same-day equity eligibility.
You are eligible to invest in SGA as a Class A shareholder from your hire date. This gives you an ownership stake in the broader SGA platform -- not just the practice you work in. As the platform grows (100+ practices, Thurston Group backing, three verticals), your equity appreciates. This is the same model that has returned $4B+ to partners across 8 Thurston Group platforms.
SGA's Individual Growth Engine exists specifically to expand your clinical scope. You are paired with a mentor who performs the procedures you want to learn -- implants, full-arch, complex restorative. The Pikos Institute gives you hands-on surgical training. One of our associates went from Class IIs to implant cases within his first 18 months.
Your mentor is an experienced SGA partner clinician. The relationship includes regular case review, chair-side observation for complex procedures, treatment planning collaboration, and career development conversations. The cadence is tailored to your needs: some associates meet weekly, others work alongside their mentor daily during their first months.
Base salary ranges from $160,000 to $240,000 depending on experience, plus a 28-32% production bonus on collections above a defined threshold. With the equity pathway, Pikos Institute access, clinical mentorship, and full benefits, the total compensation package is $37,000-$70,000 above what a comparable corporate DSO position offers.
SGA offers a 90-day mutual evaluation period. If the experience does not match what was communicated during recruiting, you can exit with no financial penalty. Signing bonuses are structured with a reasonable vesting schedule -- not a punitive clawback.
The equity pathway builds a parallel wealth-creation vehicle that a salary-only position cannot. While you pay down loans, your Class A shareholder position appreciates as the SGA platform grows. Over a 5-7 year horizon, the combination of competitive salary, production bonuses, and equity participation creates a financial trajectory that pure W-2 income at a corporate DSO cannot match.
Some locations are in smaller communities, and some are in metro areas across Arizona, California, Michigan, Minnesota, Texas, Nevada, and Florida. We match associates with locations that fit their lifestyle. Many associates who initially hesitated about smaller markets discovered lower cost of living, higher earning potential, and a pace of life that actually lets you enjoy your career.
