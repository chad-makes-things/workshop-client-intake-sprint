Who Is SGA Dental Partners?

SGA Dental Partners consolidates three Thurston Group-backed entities: SGA (community dentistry, Southeast), Gen4 Dental Partners (collaborative general and specialty, national), and MODIS Dental Partners (implantology and specialty, anchored by Dr. Michael Pikos and the Pikos Institute). SGA's deal structure is a joint-venture equity partnership: partners receive 60–70% upfront liquidity and retain 30–40% as equity in the combined SGA platform. Clinical autonomy is contractual. The Thurston Group has returned $4B+ to partners across 8 dental and healthcare platforms over 38 years.

The Specialty Focus Difference

SALT: Pediatric, Orthodontic, Oral Surgery

SALT was built from the ground up for these three specialties. Their operational support is tuned to specific needs:

  • ●Pediatric: Shorter appointments, higher volume, behavior management, heavy Medicaid insurance mix, pediatric-trained staffing
  • ●Orthodontic: Recurring treatment models, Invisalign/braces case management, long-term patient acquisition cycles
  • ●Oral surgery: Surgical suite standards, anesthesia protocols, referral network management

SALT's specialty-specific operational expertise is plausible — a DSO that only supports these specialties can develop deeper knowledge than a generalist DSO.

SGA: Implantology, Periodontics, Multi-Specialty

SGA's specialty focus lives in the MODIS vertical, built around implantology and periodontics:

  • ✓Implantology: Full-arch, zygomatic, guided surgery, same-day procedures. Dr. Michael Pikos + Dr. Grant Olson (138K YouTube subs).
  • ✓Periodontics: Traditional perio services plus implant placement and bone grafting
  • ✓Oral surgery: Represented through partners like Coastal Jaw Surgery in the MODIS network

Key structural difference: MODIS is connected to the Pikos Institute — specialty partners receive an education platform that advances their skills, not just operational support.

Where SGA Dental Partners Wins

1

The Education Platform

SALT does not operate a proprietary education institute, clinical symposium, or structured mentorship program. SGA has the Pikos Institute (hands-on implantology from fundamental to advanced zygomatic techniques), 4Front Symposium, and Individual Growth Engine. For specialists, clinical development is not optional — it is the foundation of a career.

2

Thurston Group Track Record

SGA's financial sponsor is named, transparent, and verifiable: Thurston Group, 38 years, $4B+ returned, 8 dental and healthcare platforms. SALT's financial backing is not publicly identified. For a specialist entrusting their life's work, knowing who is behind the platform matters.

3

Implantology Vertical Depth

MODIS is purpose-built for implantology: the Pikos Institute, Dr. Pikos as founding partner, Dr. Grant Olson's content platform, capital investment in specialty infrastructure (CT scanners, surgical suites, in-house labs). If implantology is your specialty, SGA/MODIS has the deepest infrastructure in the DSO market.

4

Cross-Referral Network

SGA's combination of community dentistry (SGA original), collaborative general/specialty (Gen4), and implantology (MODIS) creates cross-referral flow: general practices refer complex implant and perio cases to MODIS partners. SALT operates within its specialty lanes without an integrated general dentistry referral network.

5

Transparent Deal Terms

SGA's JV equity model is documented: 60–70% upfront, 30–40% platform equity, ongoing clinical compensation. SALT's specific deal terms are not publicly disclosed. Transparency in deal structure allows prospective partners to evaluate the financial proposition before the first conversation.

Where SALT Dental Partners Wins

1

Purpose-Built for Pediatric and Orthodontic Practices

If you run a pediatric or orthodontic practice, SALT is one of the only DSOs that truly understands your operational model — Medicaid mixes, scheduling patterns, behavior management, recurring treatment cycles. General dentistry DSOs, including SGA's general verticals, do not have the same depth.

2

De Novo Development Capability

SALT builds new locations in addition to acquiring existing practices. With 10+ new locations planned in 2025, SALT can grow into markets where no existing practice is available. SGA's primarily acquisition-driven model does not currently emphasize de novo development.

3

Rapid Growth Trajectory

SALT's 300% geographic expansion trajectory is impressive for an organization founded in 2018. Recognition by Becker's as a "Top DSO to Know in 2025" validates the momentum. More practices, more geographic diversification.

4

Multi-Brand Architecture at Scale

With 65+ affiliated brands across 20+ states, SALT clearly practices what it preaches regarding brand preservation. The visible diversity of brands on SALT's website provides tangible evidence that local identities are preserved.

5

Specialty-Peer Network Homogeneity

If being surrounded by peers who practice the same specialty matters, SALT's 233+ doctors (predominantly pediatric, ortho, and oral surgery) creates a community of like-minded specialists. SGA's network is more diverse across general and specialty practice.
