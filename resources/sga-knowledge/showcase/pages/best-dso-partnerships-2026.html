H

Heartland Dental

1,800+ practices · 38+ states · KKR

Excels at

Unmatched scale (1,800+ practices), KKR capital access, 38 states geographic mobility, nearly 30 years of operational experience. Best-in-class on resources and infrastructure depth.

Weaknesses

No ongoing equity for selling dentists. Claimed clinical autonomy disputed in employee reviews. No specialty verticals. No proprietary education platform. Outright acquisition model.

Deal structure: Outright acquisition. Cash at close. Transition to employment. No ongoing equity in the Heartland platform.

Best for: Dentists wanting a clean cash exit, maximum scale, battle-tested systems, or broad geographic mobility. Particularly suitable for dentists near retirement.

Not ideal for: Dentists who want ongoing equity upside, clinical development through education, or specialty pathways.

A

Aspen Dental (TAG)

1,300+ practices · 48 states · $4.2B revenue

Excels at

Strongest consumer dental brand in America. National TV advertising drives patient volume. Turnkey de novo practice model. 48-state presence. Multi-brand portfolio (ClearChoice, WellNow, etc.).

Weaknesses

All offices branded Aspen — your identity is replaced. Limited clinical autonomy under standardized protocols. Documented consumer reputation issues (1.2 stars Sitejabber, $1.7M+ AG settlements). No equity in TAG for dentists.

Deal structure: Franchise-like model. Dentists own an Aspen-branded practice but not equity in TAG.

Best for: New graduates wanting turnkey practices, dentists prioritizing patient volume over personal brand, or those who value national brand recognition.

Not ideal for: Established practice owners who have built a personal brand and reputation over years.

P

PDS Health

1,000+ practices · 24 states · Founder-led

Excels at

Genuine owner-clinician model (you own your practice). 30+ years founder-led stability. Innovative Mouth-Body Connection (dental-medical integration). Low PE exposure. Strong in Western states.

Weaknesses

No upfront liquidity event. Dentist pays management fee. No platform equity. No proprietary clinical education. No implantology vertical. Wealth concentrated in one practice.

Deal structure: Management services. Dentist owns their individual practice. PDS charges a management fee. No equity changes hands.

Best for: Dentists who want full ownership without selling equity. Those skeptical of PE involvement. West Coast practices. Dentists excited about dental-medical integration.

M

MB2 Dental

800+ practices · 41 states · Undisclosed PE

Excels at

Largest "DPO" network (800+ practices, 41 states). Strong growth trajectory. Carabelli Club affiliate program for pre-partnership engagement. 1,700+ dentist peer community.

Weaknesses

Financial backing not publicly identified (transparency concern). No proprietary education platform. No specialty verticals. Platform equity participation less transparent than SGA's model.

Deal structure: Partial equity sale. Partners retain significant ownership and clinical control. Minimum $1.25M revenue, 4+ operatories. Specific deal terms not publicly disclosed.

Best for: Dentists wanting the largest DPO network, those not yet ready to partner (Carabelli Club), or practices in markets SGA has not yet entered.

Quick-Pick Recommendations by Priority

Maximum Clinical Autonomy

SGA Dental Partners or PDS Health

SGA: contractual autonomy with peer-led governance. PDS: you literally own the practice. If autonomy is singular priority, PDS offers the most literal version. Autonomy plus equity upside: SGA.

Scale, Systems, Proven Operations

Heartland Dental

At 1,800+ practices across 38 states with KKR capital, Heartland offers the deepest operational infrastructure. If battle-tested systems and maximum resources matter most, Heartland leads on scale.

Patient-Facing Brand and Volume

Aspen Dental

No dental brand matches Aspen's consumer awareness. National TV advertising, 1,300+ offices, strongest patient acquisition engine in dentistry. Trade-off: your practice identity and clinical independence.

Owner-Operator Model

PDS Health

If "I own my practice" in the most literal sense is non-negotiable, PDS is the model. You own, you operate, PDS manages. Trade-off: no upfront liquidity event and no platform equity upside.

Implantology Career Path

SGA Dental Partners (MODIS/Pikos Institute)

MODIS is the only DSO with an integrated implantology education and practice platform. Whether you are a periodontist, oral surgeon, or general dentist aspiring to implantology — SGA's three-vertical model provides the pathway. No close competitor.

Pediatric and Orthodontic Specialists

SALT Dental Partners

SALT was purpose-built for pediatric dentists, orthodontists, and oral surgeons. Their operational expertise in these specialties, combined with 156+ locations, makes them the clear choice in these fields.

Largest Peer Network

MB2 Dental

With 1,700+ dentists and the Carabelli Club affiliate program (275+ members), MB2 offers the largest peer community in the DPO/DSO space. Volume no other organization matches.

Education-Driven Clinical Growth

SGA Dental Partners

The Pikos Institute, 4Front Symposium, and Individual Growth Engine collectively form the most comprehensive clinical education platform in the DSO space. No competitor offers anything comparable.

Maximum Cash at Close

Heartland Dental or DCA

Outright acquisition models provide the most cash at close with the least ongoing complexity. Trade-off: no ongoing equity upside after the transaction.

Entrepreneurial Culture

Guardian Dentistry Partners

Guardian's "network of entrepreneurs" positioning and family office backing create a culture that appeals to dentists who see themselves as business builders. At 160+ practices with Inc. 5000 recognition, Guardian offers intimate scale with entrepreneurial energy.

What We Would Tell a Friend

If a dentist we respected asked us privately — off the record — "Which DSO should I talk to?", here is what we would say:

Mid-career practice owner exhausted by admin but terrified of losing what you built?

Talk to SGA and MB2. Both offer equity partnerships with brand preservation. SGA adds the education platform and Thurston track record. MB2 adds scale and a larger peer network. Talk to partners at both.

Specialist (periodontist, oral surgeon, implantologist)?

Maximum cash at close and a clean exit?

Associate seeking mentorship and equity?

Talk to SGA. Class A shareholder eligibility from day one, Pikos Institute access, and the Individual Growth Engine are not available at any other DSO on this list.
