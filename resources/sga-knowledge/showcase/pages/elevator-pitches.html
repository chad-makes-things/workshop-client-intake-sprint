The One-Sentence Differentiator

If you can only say ONE sentence about SGA, say this

10 seconds Works for any audience
"SGA is the only dental partnership with a built-in clinical education platform -- the Pikos Institute and the 4Front Symposium -- backed by a PE firm that has done this 8 times and returned $4 billion to partners over 38 years."
What this sentence contains
Education (Pikos + 4Front) Track record (8 platforms, $4B, 38yr) Unique to SGA -- no competitor can say it

Practice Owners

Mid-career dentists considering a partnership or liquidity event

15 seconds Cocktail Party
"I work with SGA Dental Partners. We partner with practice owners who are exhausted by the business side of dentistry but do not want to lose what they built. The short version: your name stays, your team stays, you get equity in a growing platform, and our education program makes you a better clinician. We are backed by a PE firm with 38 years and $4B in partner returns. Do you know anyone in that situation?"
Problem (exhausted by business) · Differentiator (name stays, education) · Trust anchor (38yr, $4B) · Open question
30 seconds Conference
"Seven out of ten dentists who sell to a DSO end up frustrated or regretful. That is not our stat -- that is what the Dentist Advisors podcast found. SGA Dental Partners was built by people who heard that number and refused to add to it.

We are a dental partnership platform -- not a corporate DSO. Practice owners keep their name, their team, and their clinical autonomy. They get a meaningful liquidity event plus equity in a multi-state platform. What makes us different: the Pikos Institute for world-class implant education, the 4Front Symposium, and the Thurston Group -- 38 years, $4 billion returned to partners, 8 platforms.

Dentists come to SGA to get better, not just to get bought."
Problem hook (7/10 regret) · Category reframe · Three differentiators · Big idea line
60 seconds BD Call Opener
"Dr. [Name], thank you for taking the time. You have probably heard the statistics -- seven or eight out of ten dentists who sell to a DSO end up frustrated or regretful. SGA Dental Partners exists because of that number.

We are not a corporate DSO. We are a dental partnership platform built around three things no competitor offers together.

First: education -- the Pikos Institute, 4Front Symposium, and a 1:1 mentorship program. Dr. Matt Cavendish was 20 years in and learned all-on-four procedures through our program. Revenues up 16.5%, staff retention 100%. His words: 'It is fun again.'

Second: the Thurston Group. 38 years, $4B+ returned, 8 platforms.

Third: your name stays, your team stays, you retain equity.

Would it make sense to schedule 45 minutes?"
Empathy opening · Cavendish proof story · Three-pillar structure · Soft CTA

Associates

Early-career dentists looking for mentorship, equity, and a real career path

15 seconds Cocktail Party
"I work with SGA Dental Partners. We pair early-career dentists with clinical mentors, give them Pikos Institute access, and offer equity from day one -- not a vague pathway, but Class A shares. If you know any associates on a production treadmill, I'd love to connect."
Mentorship + Pikos · Immediate equity (Class A) · Production treadmill pain
30 seconds Conference
"If you are an associate right now, you probably know the feeling: 25-30 patients a day, 15 minutes each, no complex cases, no one to bounce ideas off of.

SGA Dental Partners is different. Individual Growth Engine mentorship. Pikos Institute access. 4Front Symposium with 100+ clinicians. Class A shareholder equity from day one. Your career is not a production number."
Pain identification (treadmill, isolation) · 4-point value stack · Big idea line
60 seconds BD Call Opener
"Dr. [Name], let me ask directly -- are you doing the kind of dentistry you envisioned in dental school? Are you getting mentored? Do you have a clear 5-year path?

SGA offers 1:1 clinical mentorship, Pikos Institute courses worth $6,500-$12,000, the 4Front Symposium, and equity from day one as a Class A shareholder.

One associate came in doing bread-and-butter dentistry, learned implants through the program, and now loves his profession. Would 30 minutes to walk through the specifics be useful?"
Self-reflection opening · Four proof points · Named outcome · Humble CTA

Pikos name = instant credibility · Infrastructure gap framing · Referral ask
30 seconds Conference
"If you are a specialist, you know: the full-arch market is exploding, but competing requires capital you do not have. MODIS provides $250K-$750K capital investment, dedicated recruiting, marketing modeled on Innovative Dental (138K YouTube, 3,390+ reviews), and equity in the SGA platform.

Anchored by Dr. Michael Pikos. Thurston Group: 38 years, $4B+ returned."
Infrastructure gap · Capital specificity ($250K-$750K) · Innovative Dental proof · Thurston anchor
60 seconds BD Call Opener
"Dr. [Name], you have probably seen the full-arch market accelerate. The question every specialist faces: how do I compete at that level from a solo practice?

MODIS exists for this. Co-founded with Dr. Michael Pikos. $250K-$750K capital investment. Dedicated recruiting. Marketing engine. Scope protection in writing.

Behind it: the Thurston Group, 38 years, $4B+ returned, 8 platforms. Would next week work for a detailed conversation?"
Market context · Pikos + capital + scope protection · Thurston track record · Concrete CTA

Referring Dentists

Dentists who may not partner themselves but can refer colleagues or patients

15 seconds Cocktail Party
"I work with SGA Dental Partners -- we are a dental partnership platform backed by a PE firm with 38 years and $4 billion in partner returns. If you know any practice owners who are burning out on the business side but do not want to lose their name, team, or autonomy -- or any associates who feel stuck on a production treadmill -- I would love an introduction. We treat referrals like gold."
Credibility anchor (38yr, $4B) · Two referral categories · Explicit appreciation
30 seconds Conference
"Quick question -- do you know any colleagues who are drowning in the business side of dentistry? The ones who are still great clinicians but spent last Sunday night doing payroll instead of being with their family?

SGA Dental Partners is a dental partnership platform where practice owners keep their name, their team, and their clinical autonomy. They get a liquidity event, equity in a growing platform, and access to the Pikos Institute and 4Front Symposium. Backed by the Thurston Group -- 38 years, $4 billion returned.

I am not asking you to sell anything. If someone comes to mind, would you be open to making an introduction?"
Sunday night pain (mental Rolodex activation) · Low-pressure referral ask
60 seconds BD Call / Peer Conversation
"Dr. [Name], I wanted to reach out because you have a reputation in the dental community here as someone people trust. That matters more than you probably realize.

I work with SGA Dental Partners. We partner with practice owners who want a liquidity event and operational relief without losing their identity. Name stays, team stays, equity in our platform, and a clinical education ecosystem -- Pikos Institute, 4Front Symposium, and 1:1 mentorship -- that genuinely makes them better clinicians. Backed by the Thurston Group: 38 years, $4B+ returned, 8 platforms.

If you hear a colleague mention they are overwhelmed by the business side, thinking about succession planning, or frustrated they cannot invest in technology -- would you be willing to pass along my name?

And for your own practice: if you refer patients who need full-arch implant work, our MODIS partners include some of the best implantologists in the country. That cross-referral relationship benefits your patients directly."
Lead with flattery · Referral mechanism as peer service · Cross-referral value (MODIS) · Low-commitment CTA

Quick Reference: Pitch Selection Guide

Match the situation to the right script

Situation Length Audience Key to Nail
Random introduction at a party 15s Owner or Referring End with a question, not a claim
Conference booth conversation 30s Owner, Associate, or Specialist Lead with the problem, not SGA
Warm BD call with practice owner 60s Owner Cavendish proof + Thurston + humble CTA
Recruiting conversation with associate 60s Associate IGE + Pikos + equity + "not a production number"
Specialist outreach call 60s Specialist Pikos + capital + scope protection + recruiting
Asking for referrals 30s 60s Referring Low pressure, high respect, cross-referral value

Memorize These 5 Things

  1. 1. The opening hook for each audience (first sentence)
  2. 2. Three differentiators: Education, Thurston, Structure
  3. 3. Cavendish proof story (20yr, all-on-four, 16.5%, "fun again")
  4. 4. Big idea: "Dentists come to SGA to get better, not just to get bought"
  5. 5. Closing CTA: "Would it make sense to schedule 45 minutes..."

Practice Tips

  • — Deliver each pitch to a timer. The 15s must actually be 15 seconds.
  • — Record yourself. Listen for corporate jargon and filler words.
  • — Practice the 60s until you can deliver it with eye contact.
  • — Role-play objections. Common: "How are you different from Heartland / MB2?"
