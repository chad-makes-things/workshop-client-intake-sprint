Where We Are Honest About What Others Offer

We believe in transparency. Here is where certain competitors may have advantages over SGA.

Scale and Geographic Reach

Heartland (1,800+), Aspen (1,300+), PDS (1,000+), and MB2 (800+) all have larger footprints. If being part of the biggest network is your primary criterion, SGA is not the largest. We are growing deliberately -- we would rather have 100 practices that fit our culture than 800 that dilute it.

National Brand Recognition

Aspen Dental is the most recognized consumer dental brand. If you want patients who search for a national name, Aspen provides that. SGA keeps your local brand -- patient acquisition depends on your reputation and SGA's centralized marketing. For most owners, that is a feature. But it is worth knowing.

Pure Practice Ownership (No Dilution)

PDS Health's owner-clinician model is the only major DSO where you retain 100% ownership of your practice. The tradeoff: no platform equity, no "second bite of the apple," and no upside beyond your own practice's performance. For some dentists, that tradeoff is worth it.

Sheer Volume of Support Staff

Heartland's 1,800-practice infrastructure means deeper bench strength in procurement, HR, legal, and technology. We would argue that having 100+ peers who know your name is more valuable than 1,800 practices where you are a number.

We share this because trust is built on honesty. If a competitor is genuinely better for your situation, we would rather you know that now than discover it after signing.

Three Ways to Think About the Decision

vs. Heartland / DCA

Outright Acquisition Model

They buy your practice. You become an employee. Cash at close, no ongoing equity. Your upside ends the day the wire hits. SGA makes you an equity partner. As the platform grows, your equity appreciates. That is the "second bite" Heartland cannot offer.

vs. PDS

Management Services Model

PDS charges you management fees. You own your practice. No equity in a larger platform. No upside beyond your four walls. SGA makes you an equity partner in a $350M+ platform. There is a difference between paying for support and owning a piece of something.

vs. MB2

Partial Equity DPO Model

MB2 is the closest structural comparison. Similar JV model. The differences: PE track record (Thurston 38yr vs undisclosed). Education (Pikos + 4Front vs none). Verticals (3 vs general only). Content (138K subscribers vs minimal). "DPO" is branding, not structure.
