Company Overview

Who we are and what we do

Company Boilerplate For press releases and media mentions

SGA Dental Partners is a national dental partnership platform that unifies community dentistry, collaborative general and specialty practice, and advanced implantology under one clinician-led organization. Backed by the Thurston Group -- a private equity firm with a 38-year track record, $4B+ returned to partners, and 8+ dental and healthcare platforms built -- SGA offers practice owners a meaningful liquidity event, full operational support, and retained equity in a growing multi-state platform while preserving their practice name, team, and clinical autonomy.

The SGA platform includes Gen4 Dental Partners (collaborative general and specialty dentistry), MODIS Dental Partners (implantology and specialty practice anchored by the Pikos Institute), and the original SGA community dentistry network spanning the Southeast. Together, these verticals serve 100+ partner practices across 10+ states.

SGA Dental Partners is headquartered in Richmond Hill, Georgia. For more information, visit sgadental.com.

Mission

To build a dental partnership platform where dentists come to get better -- not just get bought. SGA preserves what practice owners built while connecting them to a network of high-performing peers, world-class education, and the operational infrastructure to practice at their best.

Tagline

"Clinical freedom surrounded by the best of everything."

The Thurston Group

SGA's financial sponsor is the Thurston Group, founded in 1988 by Patrick J. Haynes III. Over 38 years, Thurston has returned over $4 billion to partners across 8+ dental and healthcare platforms including Smile Doctors, US Endodontics Partners, US Oral Surgery Management, and ARC Health. Thurston's model is patient capital -- building for long-term value, not quick-flip returns.
