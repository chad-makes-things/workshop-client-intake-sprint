SGA Dental Partners vs MB2 Dental

The two closest competitors in the dental partnership space — similar language, different realities.

DSO vs DPO PE Transparency Education Platform Scale vs Depth

TL;DR

MB2 Dental and SGA Dental Partners are the two organizations most directly competing for the same dentist: the mid-career practice owner who wants a partnership — not an acquisition — with retained equity and clinical autonomy. MB2 calls itself a "Dental Partnership Organization" (DPO) with 800+ practices across 41 states and the tagline "Power to the Partners." SGA calls itself the 4th-generation DSO, with 60–125+ practices, three clinical verticals, and the Thurston Group behind it ($4B+ returned, 38 years). The differentiator is not the language — it is what happens after you sign. Choose MB2 if you want the largest DPO network. Choose SGA if you want clinical development, specialty pathways, and a PE sponsor whose dental track record you can verify down to the dollar.

On "Dentist-Founded" Claims

MB2: Founded in 2007 by Dr. Reza Khorashahi ("Dr. K") and a group of dentists in Texas. The founding story is genuine — dentists built MB2 because they wanted an alternative to corporate DSOs. The "dentist-founded" DNA is authentic.

SGA/Gen4: Gen4 was co-founded by Dr. Mitch Ellingson, a practicing dentist and current CCO. MODIS was built around Dr. Michael Pikos. The key question is not who was founded by a dentist — it is whether the clinician voice still shapes decisions today at scale. At SGA's current size, Dr. Ellingson still makes clinical advisory decisions and Dr. Pikos still teaches at the institute that bears his name. Scale changes the proximity between founding vision and daily reality.

S

Who Is SGA Dental Partners?

SGA Dental Partners consolidates three Thurston Group-backed entities: SGA (community dentistry, Southeast), Gen4 Dental Partners (collaborative general and specialty, national), and MODIS Dental Partners (implantology and specialty, anchored by Dr. Michael Pikos and the Pikos Institute). The model is a joint-venture equity partnership: selling dentists receive 60–70% upfront liquidity while retaining 30–40% as equity in the combined SGA platform. Clinical autonomy is contractual. The Thurston Group has returned $4B+ to partners across 8 dental and healthcare platforms over 38 years.

Who Is MB2 Dental?

MB2 Dental was founded in 2007 and has grown into the self-described "largest Dental Partnership Organization (DPO)" in the United States. With 800+ practices across 41 states (milestone reached September 2025) and 1,700+ dentists in its network, MB2 has experienced rapid growth. MB2's positioning is explicitly anti-DSO: they coined "DPO" to create categorical distance from traditional dental support organizations. The tagline is "Power to the Partners." MB2 also operates the Carabelli Club — an affiliate and networking program with 275+ members — as an on-ramp for dentists not yet ready to partner.

The Transparency Difference

SGA: Named, Verifiable, Contactable

  • PE Firm: Thurston Group
  • Founder: Patrick J. Haynes III (38 years)
  • Managing Director: Daniel F. Davis
  • Returns: $4B+ returned to partners
  • Prior platforms: Smile Doctors, US Endodontics Partners, US Oral Surgery Management, ARC Health, US Orthopaedic Partners
  • Deal structure: Documented — 60–70% upfront, 30–40% equity

MB2: Opaque Financial Structure

MB2's financial sponsors and ownership structure are not publicly identified. Specific deal multiples, equity split percentages, and the identity of financial sponsors are not in public materials.

Why this matters: The entity behind the partnership determines what happens during hold periods, at recapitalization events, and at eventual exits. When dentists report regret after DSO sales, the financial structure is often the source. Knowing who is behind the platform is not a nice-to-have — it is due diligence.

Where SGA Dental Partners Wins

1

The Education Platform

MB2 has no proprietary education platform. SGA has the Pikos Institute, 4Front Symposium, and Individual Growth Engine. Dr. Matt Cavendish: 20 years in practice, complacent, then paired with a mentor. Revenues up 16.5%. "It is fun again." MB2 provides business support. SGA provides business support AND clinical growth.

2

Specialty Verticals and Implantology Pathway

MB2 is a general dentistry platform. SGA operates three verticals. A general dentist in SGA can develop implant skills through the Pikos Institute. A periodontist can access MODIS's specialty infrastructure. MB2 does not have a dedicated specialty vertical or structured career pathways.

3

PE Track Record Transparency

The Thurston Group is named, its principals are identified, its track record is quantified, and its prior platform partners can be contacted. MB2's financial backing is not publicly identified. For a dentist entrusting their life's work, transparency about who is behind the curtain is not an unreasonable expectation.

4

Peer-Led Clinical Governance

SGA's clinical advisory board is led by practicing dentists, including CCO Dr. Mitch Ellingson. The "dentists teaching dentists" culture provides structural protection for clinical autonomy. At 800+ practices, MB2's governance model is necessarily more distributed and less visible.

5

Clinical Thought Leadership

SGA has Dr. Grant Olson (138K YouTube subscribers, 23M+ views), Dr. Michael Pikos (globally recognized), and the MODIS Community Conversations podcast. MB2 has a corporate blog. In a market where clinical credibility matters, SGA's thought leadership bench is deeper.

Where MB2 Dental Wins

1

Scale and Geographic Reach

800+ practices across 41 states is substantial. MB2's footprint means presence in most US markets, providing geographic diversity, operational depth, and purchasing power SGA's current 60–125 practices cannot yet match.

2

Rapid Growth Trajectory

From founding in 2007 to 800+ practices in 2025, MB2's growth rate demonstrates market demand for the DPO model. That trajectory creates network effects — more practices means more shared knowledge, more purchasing leverage, and more career mobility.

3

The Carabelli Club

MB2's affiliate program (275+ members in its first year) creates an on-ramp for dentists not yet ready to fully partner. SGA does not have an equivalent pre-partnership program. This is smart positioning that builds relationships over time.

4

Largest Peer Network in the DPO Space

With 1,700+ dentists in the network, MB2 offers one of the largest peer communities in the DSO/DPO space. Volume of peers can create diverse learning, cross-referral potential, and professional networking opportunities.

5

The "DPO" Brand Distinction

MB2 coined "DPO" to differentiate from the DSO label. Whether or not the structural differences are significant, the branding is effective. First-mover advantage on terminology matters in a market where "DSO" carries baggage.

Deciding Factors for a Mid-Career Practice Owner

If you are 15–25 years in, doing $1.5M–$3M in revenue, both MB2 and SGA are viable options. Here is how to decide:

Clinical growth or operational support only?

Clinical growth: SGA. If your CE needs are met and you just need operational relief: either.

Who is behind the PE investment?

Transparency matters: SGA. Comfortable with opacity: MB2 is fine.

Specialty ambitions?

Implantology / specialty: SGA. General dentistry only: either.

Network size vs. network depth?

Size: MB2. Depth of engagement: SGA.
