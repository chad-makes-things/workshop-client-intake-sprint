Strategic Overview

The landscape, the opportunity, and the approach

Current State: Green-Field Opportunity

SGA's current website (sgadental.com) has minimal content depth -- essentially a brochure site with no blog, no resource center, and no content targeting high-intent keywords. Domain authority is low single digits. This is actually an advantage: the consolidation of SGA, Gen4, and MODIS under one brand lets us build content architecture from scratch, targeting keyword gaps that even large DSO competitors have left open.

Capture Intent

Target high-intent keywords where dentists are actively researching practice transitions, DSO partnerships, and career decisions. These searches signal readiness to act.

Build Authority

Establish topical authority through hub-and-spoke content clusters. Every cluster has a pillar page, 5-8 supporting articles, and cross-links that signal expertise to Google.

Dominate Local

Dental is inherently local. Optimize Google Business Profiles, build location pages for every practice, and capture "dentist near me" searches across 10+ states.

Internal Linking Rules

1

Every page gets at least 3 internal inbound links. No orphan pages allowed.

2

Pillar pages receive links from all spokes and pass equity to landing pages.

3

Cross-cluster bridges limited to 2-3 per article to maintain topical relevance.

4

No page more than 3 clicks from the homepage. Flat site architecture.

5

Vary anchor text: 40% primary keyword, 60% natural variations.

6

Priority pages (/partners, /careers, /implants) receive 15-30 inbound links each.

Technical SEO Priorities

Infrastructure that enables search visibility

Structured Data (Schema)

  • - Organization schema on homepage
  • - LocalBusiness (Dentist) on each location page
  • - FAQPage schema on /partners and /implants
  • - Article schema on all blog posts
  • - JobPosting schema on career pages
  • - Person schema for leadership team
  • - BreadcrumbList sitewide

Core Web Vitals

  • - LCP under 2.5 seconds (image optimization, CDN)
  • - INP under 200ms (minimize JS blocking)
  • - CLS under 0.1 (image dimensions, font display:swap)
  • - WebP/AVIF image formats with srcset
  • - Lazy loading below-fold images
  • - Preconnect to font and analytics origins

Crawlability & Indexing

  • - XML sitemap with auto-update on publish
  • - Robots.txt: allow all public pages
  • - Canonical tags on every page
  • - 301 redirects: gen4dental.com and modisdental.com to sgadental.com
  • - Hreflang (if multi-language needed later)
  • - Fix duplicate title tags (current "Home - SGA..." issue)

On-Page Optimization

  • - Unique title tags: keyword + brand (under 60 chars)
  • - Meta descriptions: CTA-oriented (under 160 chars)
  • - One H1 per page with primary keyword
  • - H2/H3 hierarchy matching search intent
  • - Alt text on all images (descriptive, keyword-aware)
  • - E-E-A-T signals: author bios, credentials, dates

Local SEO Playbook

1
Google Business Profile Optimization

Claim and optimize every practice GBP. Complete business info, categories (Dentist, Dental Implants Provider, Pediatric Dentist as applicable), photos, hours, services, and Q&A. Post weekly updates.

2
Location Pages on sgadental.com

Build unique, content-rich pages for each practice: /practices/[practice-name]-[city]-[state]. Include doctor bios, services, patient reviews, embedded map, photos, and FAQ specific to that location.

3
Citation Building & NAP Consistency

Ensure Name, Address, Phone (NAP) data is identical across Google, Yelp, Healthgrades, Zocdoc, WebMD, YellowPages, and 50+ directories. Audit quarterly.

4
Review Generation Program

Implement post-visit review requests via SMS/email. Target 50+ reviews per location. Respond to every review within 24 hours. Innovative Dental already leads here with 3,390+ reviews.

5
Local Content & Community Signals

Publish location-specific blog posts, community event sponsorships, and local news features. Build local backlinks from chambers of commerce, schools, and community organizations.
