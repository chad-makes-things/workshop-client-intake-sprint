**Asset type:** Multi-channel promotion strategy for the Dental Partnership Readiness Scorecard

**Secondary reach:** Dr. Priya Nair (associate -- enters Foundation Builder tier, routed to DR funnel)

---

## Promotion Strategy Overview

The scorecard is a top-of-funnel conversion mechanism. Its job is to convert anonymous traffic and passive awareness into identified, segmented leads with rich data profiles. Every promotion channel answers one question: "Where are practice owners and specialists already spending attention, and how do we intercept that attention with a diagnostic offer that feels valuable, not salesy?"

The Sage archetype governs all promotion: SGA is the wise evaluator offering insight, not the aggressive seller demanding attention. Copy leads with diagnostic curiosity ("Where does your practice stand?") rather than transactional urgency ("Find out how much your practice is worth!").

## Channel 6: Conference and Event Promotion

### In-Person Strategy

The scorecard becomes a conversation tool at dental conferences (Dykema, Hinman, state dental association meetings, AAP, AO, Pikos Symposium).

**Booth integration:**
- Tablet stations at the SGA booth where attendees can take the scorecard on-site
- QR code on all printed materials linking to the scorecard
- Booth staff trained to introduce the scorecard as a diagnostic, not a sales qualifier: "Before we talk about anything else, this 5-minute assessment will give you a clear picture of where your practice stands. The results are yours -- no obligation."

**Hosted dinner integration:**
- Include scorecard QR code in dinner invitations
- Reference scorecard results during dinner conversation: "A lot of the owners who take our diagnostic score highest in clinical growth and lowest in operational independence. How would you score on that?"

**Post-event email:**
Subject: We met at [Event] -- here is the scorecard we mentioned
Body: Brief recap of the scorecard, link to take it, note that results are confidential.

### Expected ROI

At a $10,000/month spend generating ~250 completed scorecards:
- ~65 Partnership Primed or Strategic Acquirer leads per month
- If 15% of Partnership Primed leads book a discovery call = ~10 calls/month
- If 30% of calls advance to site visit = ~3 site visits/month
- If 40% of site visits convert to LOI = ~1.2 new partnerships/month

At an average deal value of $3M-$8M, the scorecard funnel pays for its annual cost ($120K) with a single closed partnership -- which has an expected deal value 25-65x the annual marketing spend.

## Launch Sequence

### Week 1: Soft Launch
- Deploy scorecard on sgadental.com/scorecard
- Send email blast to existing database

### Week 2: Paid Activation
- Launch LinkedIn Thought Leader Ads (Variation 1)
- Launch Facebook ads (Variation 1)
- Deploy exit-intent popup and sidebar widget on sgadental.com

### Week 3: Expand and Test
- Launch LinkedIn Variation 2 and Facebook Variation 2
- Publish organic Facebook posts
- Enable retargeting ads for scorecard page visitors who did not complete

### Week 4: Optimize
- Review first 200+ completions for tier distribution
- Adjust questions if too many or too few land in Partnership Primed/Strategic Acquirer
- Pause underperforming ad variants
- Scale winning variants by 25%

### Ongoing
- Monthly performance review (completions, tier distribution, email sequence engagement, discovery calls booked)
- Quarterly creative refresh (new ad copy, new organic posts)
- Conference integration at every event
- Podcast CTA at every appearance
