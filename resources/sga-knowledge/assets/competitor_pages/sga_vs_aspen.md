## TL;DR

Aspen Dental (part of The Aspen Group) is the most recognized consumer-facing dental brand in America, with 1,300+ offices across 48 states, a multi-brand portfolio including ClearChoice implant centers, and massive marketing spend driving patient volume. SGA Dental Partners is a joint-venture equity partnership platform backed by the Thurston Group, with an integrated education platform and three clinical verticals including the MODIS implantology division anchored by the Pikos Institute. These two organizations serve fundamentally different visions of what a dental partnership should look like. Choose Aspen if you want a turnkey branded practice with built-in patient demand. Choose SGA if you want to keep your name, own equity in a growing platform, and develop clinically alongside peers.

## Who Is Aspen Dental?

Aspen Dental, operating under The Aspen Group (TAG), was founded in 1998 and has become the most visible dental brand in America. With 1,300+ offices across 48 states, approximately 5,300+ clinicians, and $4.2B in annualized net revenue (first half 2025, 8% year-over-year growth), Aspen is the second-largest DSO in the United States.

TAG's portfolio extends beyond general dentistry: it includes ClearChoice (dental implant centers), WellNow Urgent Care, Chapter Aesthetic Studio, and Lovet Pet Health Care. The brand positions itself around patient access, with campaigns like "The Yes Campaign" and messaging centered on "breaking down barriers to ensure everyone gets the care they need."

Aspen's model is distinctive among DSOs. Dentists operate branded Aspen Dental offices under standardized protocols, systems, and marketing. Aspen builds new practices (de novo development) in addition to acquiring existing ones. It is, in many ways, closer to a franchise model than a traditional DSO partnership.

## Who Is SGA Dental Partners?

SGA Dental Partners is a dental partnership platform built on a joint-venture equity model. Backed by the Thurston Group (38 years, $4B+ returned to partners across 8 dental and healthcare platforms), SGA consolidates three complementary verticals under one umbrella:

- **SGA (original):** Community dentistry network across the Southeast
- **Gen4 Dental Partners:** Collaborative general and specialty practices nationally
- **MODIS Dental Partners:** Implantology and specialty, anchored by Dr. Michael Pikos and the Pikos Institute

Partner dentists receive upfront liquidity while retaining equity in the combined platform. Practices keep their names, teams, and clinical protocols. The model is explicitly designed for dentists who want to remain owners -- not employees of a branded corporate entity.

## The Implant Comparison: ClearChoice vs MODIS/Pikos

For dentists with implantology ambitions -- or those evaluating which platform offers the strongest implant pathway -- this comparison deserves its own section.

### ClearChoice (Aspen/TAG)

ClearChoice is a network of dedicated implant centers acquired by TAG. It operates as a separate brand from Aspen Dental, with its own locations, branding, and patient experience. ClearChoice focuses on full-arch (All-on-4) implant procedures and has strong consumer brand awareness through direct-to-patient advertising.

**Strengths:** Established consumer brand in implants. Proven patient acquisition model. Dedicated implant centers with specialized workflows.

**Limitations:** ClearChoice is a separate silo. General Aspen dentists do not have an educational pathway into ClearChoice implantology. There is no integrated clinical development pipeline where a general dentist can upskill into implant procedures. ClearChoice is a referral destination, not a growth pathway.

### MODIS/Pikos Institute (SGA)

MODIS is SGA's implantology-focused vertical, with Dr. Michael Pikos -- a pioneer in dental implantology and globally recognized educator -- as a founding partner. The Pikos Institute provides hands-on implantology training ranging from fundamental courses to advanced zygomatic implant techniques.

**Strengths:** Integrated education and clinical pathway. General dentists within SGA can develop implant skills through the Pikos Institute without leaving the organization. Dr. Grant Olson (138K YouTube subscribers, 23M+ views, one of 300 CEREC Mentors worldwide) exemplifies the clinical innovation culture. MODIS partners receive capital investment for specialty infrastructure -- CT scanners, surgical suites, in-house labs.

**Limitations:** Smaller scale than ClearChoice. Fewer implant-dedicated locations. Less consumer brand awareness for the implant-specific patient.

**The distinction:** ClearChoice is a branded service you refer patients to. MODIS/Pikos is an education platform that develops you into the clinician who performs those procedures. If your goal is to grow your implantology capabilities, SGA's integrated model creates a pathway ClearChoice does not.

## Deal Structure Comparison

### Aspen Dental

Aspen operates what is effectively a franchise model. Dentists become practice owners under the Aspen Dental brand, with standardized protocols, marketing, and operations. The practice is branded Aspen -- not your name. Aspen builds or acquires the location, and the dentist operates within the Aspen system.

This means high patient volume from day one (Aspen's national marketing drives demand), but limited independence in how you practice, brand yourself, or evolve your clinical approach. You own an Aspen Dental practice -- you do not own equity in The Aspen Group.

### SGA Dental Partners

SGA's deal is a joint-venture equity partnership:

- **60-70% upfront liquidity** at close
- **30-40% retained as equity** in the SGA platform
- **Clinical compensation** continues (base + production bonus)
- **Your name stays.** Dental Partners of SW Georgia is still Dental Partners of SW Georgia. Periodontal Associates of Memphis is still Periodontal Associates of Memphis.
- **Your equity grows** as SGA's platform appreciates

The Thurston Group's $4B+ returned to partners across 8 dental and healthcare platforms is the historical evidence behind this equity model.

### The Fundamental Difference

Aspen gives you a branded business with built-in demand. SGA gives you ownership in a growing platform while you keep the business you already built. These are philosophically opposite approaches. Neither is wrong -- but they serve different dentists with different goals.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Aspen Dental a franchise?

Not technically -- Aspen Dental does not operate under a franchise disclosure document. But the model functions similarly. Dentists operate branded Aspen offices with standardized systems, marketing, and protocols. You own an Aspen-branded practice, not an independent practice with Aspen's support. SGA's model is structurally different: your practice retains its identity, and you own equity in the broader SGA platform.

### How does Aspen's ClearChoice compare to SGA's MODIS/Pikos Institute?

ClearChoice is a network of dedicated implant centers -- a separate brand from Aspen with its own marketing and patient flow. It is a referral destination. MODIS/Pikos is an integrated implantology vertical within SGA that includes a world-class education institute (Pikos Institute), capital investment in specialty infrastructure, and a clinical development pathway where general dentists can upskill into implant procedures. ClearChoice treats implant patients. MODIS/Pikos develops implant clinicians.

### What about Aspen's reputation issues?

Aspen has faced significant consumer complaints (4,100+ on PissedConsumer, 1.2 stars on Sitejabber), multiple state Attorney General investigations, and $1.7M+ in settlements related to allegations of deceptive practices and overtreatment. These are public, documented issues. SGA's model -- where practices retain their own names and reputations, and clinical decisions remain with the practitioner -- is structurally designed to avoid the production-pressure dynamics that drive these complaints.

### I am a mid-career practice owner with 20 years invested. Which is right for me?

If you have spent 20 years building a practice with your name on it, Aspen is likely not the right fit -- because Aspen replaces your brand with theirs. SGA's model was explicitly designed for your situation: you keep your name, retain equity in a growing platform, get operational relief, and join a peer community of experienced clinicians. As Dr. Mitchel Godat (Periodontal Associates of Memphis) put it: "SGA supports us and manages things behind the scenes. Our partnership allows us to focus on changing lives."

### Can I keep my staff if I join Aspen vs SGA?

Both organizations retain existing staff during transitions. However, SGA publishes an 85% staff retention rate across its network -- a metric no other DSO makes public. SGA's onboarding process is designed to make the transition invisible to staff and patients: benefits are upgraded (Cigna, 401(k) through Voya, PTO), operational burden shifts to SGA's back office, and the practice continues operating under its established identity.

### What is Aspen's associate dentist model compared to SGA's?

Aspen hires associates into branded offices with competitive compensation and high patient volume. SGA offers associates the Individual Growth Engine (1:1 clinical mentorship), Pikos Institute access, 4Front Symposium participation, and Class A shareholder eligibility from day one. The difference: Aspen gives you a job with patients. SGA gives you a career with a growth trajectory and an equity pathway.
