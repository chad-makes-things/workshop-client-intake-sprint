# SGA Dental Partners -- Content Repurposing Matrix

**Prepared:** March 2026
**Purpose:** Map how each pillar content piece breaks into platform-specific assets using a systematic 1-to-many framework.
**Content Pillars:** Clinical Education, Partner Stories, Industry Insights, Behind-the-Scenes, The SGA Difference
**Source Platform:** YouTube (Dr. Grant Olson's Innovative Dental channel, 138K subscribers)
**Distribution Platforms:** YouTube, LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, Email, Blog

---

## The Content Pyramid

```
PILLAR CONTENT
(1 long-form YouTube video or blog post per week)
               |
               v
    -------------------------
    |           |           |
    v           v           v
 PLATFORM   PLATFORM   PLATFORM
 ADAPTATIONS ADAPTATIONS ADAPTATIONS
 (5-10 pieces per pillar)
               |
               v
    -------------------------
    |           |           |
    v           v           v
  EMAIL     COMMUNITY   PAID ADS
  EXCERPTS  ENGAGEMENT  CREATIVE
  (1-2)     (ongoing)   (2-3)
```

One pillar piece produces 15-25 platform-specific assets. Two pillar pieces per week yield 30-50 total weekly content items across all platforms.

## Content Recycling Strategy

High-performing content should be recycled, not retired. The recycling cadence:

| Content Type | Recycle After | Recycling Method |
|-------------|--------------|-----------------|
| Transformation Reels | 60 days | Re-post with updated caption and fresh music/edit |
| Educational Carousels | 90 days | Redesign with updated data/stats |
| Partner Story clips | 90 days | Re-edit with different hook or angle |
| Thought leadership posts | 120 days | Rewrite with new angle or updated context |
| Blog posts | 6 months | Update with current year data, re-promote |
| Evergreen Shorts | 45 days | Re-post (algorithms treat re-uploads as new content on Shorts platforms) |

**Rule:** Any piece that exceeds 2x the average engagement rate is a "winner" and enters the recycle queue. Winners get re-posted, re-edited, and re-distributed before any new content is created.
