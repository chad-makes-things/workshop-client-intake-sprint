## Production Notes

- **Reel production:** All Reels are extracted from Dr. Olson's YouTube long-form content. The video editor creates vertical (9:16) exports with burned-in captions during the YouTube Shorts editing workflow. These same files serve TikTok, IG Reels, and FB Reels.
- **Carousel design:** Use Canva or Figma templates with consistent SGA brand palette. Each carousel should take 30-45 minutes to produce once the template is established.
- **Native uploads:** ALWAYS upload natively to each platform. Never cross-post directly from Instagram to Facebook or vice versa. Each platform's algorithm penalizes reshared content. Export clean files for each platform.
- **Caption length:** Instagram captions can be long (the algorithm does not penalize length). Facebook captions should be slightly shorter -- front-load the hook in the first 2 lines before the "See More" fold.
- **Hashtag strategy:** Instagram: 5-8 targeted hashtags per post (mix of broad and niche). Facebook: 0-2 hashtags (Facebook's algorithm does not weight them the same way).
- **Paid boost candidates:** Days 1, 7, 10, 16, 22, 29, 30 (transformation Reels and conversion posts) are the highest-ROI candidates for paid boost. Start with $50-$100 per boost targeting adults 35-65 within 15 miles of practice locations.
