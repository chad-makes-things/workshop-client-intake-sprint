## 1. Webinar Topic & Title Options

### Option A: Educational (Sage Archetype -- Recommended)

**"The Dental Partnership Decision: What 100+ Practice Owners Learned After Saying Yes"**

Rationale: Positions SGA as the educator, not the seller. The number "100+" anchors authority. "After Saying Yes" creates curiosity about post-deal reality -- the #1 messaging gap in the DSO market. Targets Schwartz Level 3-4 (solution-aware, comparing options) by promising insider knowledge from practitioners who have already crossed the bridge.

### Option B: Curiosity-Driven

**"7 Out of 10 Dentists Regret Selling to a DSO. Here's What the Other 3 Did Differently."**

Rationale: Leads with the market's most visceral statistic (from Dentist Advisors podcast). The curiosity gap is powerful -- every practice owner googling DSO partnerships has encountered regret stories but never a structured framework for what the "happy 30%" did right. Slightly more aggressive tone; best for paid traffic where pattern interrupt matters.

### Option C: Result-Driven

**"The 7-Question Framework That Predicts Whether You'll Be in the Happy 30% or the Miserable 70% After Partnering with a DSO"**

Rationale: Promises a tangible tool (framework) they can use immediately. The "7 questions" structure is concrete and memorable. Implicitly positions SGA as having the answer key. Best for audiences already deep in evaluation mode (retargeting, warm email list, conference attendees).

### Recommended Title

**Option A** for the primary campaign. It balances educational authority with curiosity and does not telegraph a sales pitch. The word "Decision" signals that this is for people actively weighing options -- filtering out tire-kickers while attracting high-intent prospects.

For paid traffic variants and retargeting, use Option B as the headline on ads driving to the registration page, with Option A as the webinar title they see after clicking. This creates a two-stage curiosity loop.

## 10. Replay Strategy & Urgency Mechanics

### Replay Tiers

| Tier | Timing | Format | Purpose |
|------|--------|--------|---------|
| Full replay | Day 0-3 (72 hours) | Complete webinar recording | Capture no-shows and attendees who want to re-watch |
| Edited replay | Day 2 | Teaching section only (22 min), pitch removed | Lower-friction format for people who resist long recordings |
| Cliff notes | Day 4 | Text-based key frameworks in email body | Capture the reading-preference segment |

### Urgency Mechanics (All Authentic)

1. **Replay expiration (72 hours):** The full replay comes down after 72 hours. This is real -- the link is deactivated. Stated in every replay email.

2. **Partner conversation window (48 hours):** The 1-on-1 conversation with a current SGA partner is available for 48 hours post-webinar. Real constraint: partner dentists batch their availability around each webinar event. After the window, they return to full clinical schedules.

3. **Post-deadline email:** Send a "window closed" confirmation on Day 3 (Attendee Email 4 above). This builds credibility for future events because the audience learns the deadlines are real.

4. **What is NOT used:** No fake countdown timers. No "only X spots left" when there is no limit. No secret extensions after publicly closing. No continuous "limited time" offers.

## 13. Post-Webinar Conversion Path

The webinar is a C.A.C. funnel authority amplifier. The conversion path post-webinar:

```
WEBINAR (authority amplifier)
    |
    v
ATTENDEE SEQUENCE (5 emails, Days 0-5)
    |
    |--> Books partner conversation (48-hr window)
    |       |
    |       v
    |    1-ON-1 PARTNER CALL (peer conversation, no BD)
    |       |
    |       v
    |    DISCOVERY CALL (with SGA business development)
    |       |
    |       v
    |    SITE VISIT + PARTNER INTRODUCTIONS
    |       |
    |       v
    |    LOI + DUE DILIGENCE + SIGNING
    |
    |--> Does NOT book within 48 hours
            |
            v
         TRANSITION TO PA-ENGAGEMENT SEQUENCE
         (8 emails, Days 8-35 per funnel architecture)
         Biweekly nurture thereafter (6-18 month cycle)
```

### Tags Applied

| Action | Tag | Consequence |
|--------|-----|-------------|
