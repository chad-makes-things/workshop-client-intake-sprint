# Instagram Ad Campaigns -- SGA Dental Partners

**Prepared:** March 2026
**Platform:** Instagram (Feed, Stories, Reels)
**Audiences:** Associate Dentists (Dr. Priya Nair), Implant Patients (Linda Dawson)
**Campaign Objectives:** Lead Generation (recruitment), Conversions (patient consultation bookings)
**Brand Voice:** Sage archetype for recruitment (career-forward, growth-oriented). Warm and human for patients (un-clinical, identity-focused).

---

## Campaign Structure Overview

### Campaign 1: Associate Dentist Recruitment (Dr. Priya Nair)
- **Objective:** Lead Generation (application submissions)
- **Budget:** $1,500-$2,500/month
- **Optimization Event:** Lead form submissions, career page visits
- **Bid Strategy:** Lowest cost initially; cost cap after 30 conversions

### Campaign 2: Implant Patient Acquisition (Linda Dawson)
- **Objective:** Conversions (virtual consultation bookings)

## A/B TEST PLAN

### Test 1: Format Performance -- Associate Recruitment

| Element | Variation A | Variation B |
|---------|------------|------------|
| **Format** | Feed Image (Variation 1 "Tooth Mechanic") | Reels Video (Variation 3 "Mentorship Story") |
| **Hypothesis** | Reels will generate 3-5x more reach and impressions (algorithmic advantage), but Feed Image will generate higher click-through and application rates (higher-intent format). |
| **Success Metric** | Reach > 10K (Reels), Application starts > 10/week (Feed) |
| **Duration** | 14 days, matched budgets |

### Test 2: Rational vs. Emotional -- Associate Recruitment

| Element | Variation A | Variation B |
|---------|------------|------------|
| **Angle** | Emotional/Identity (Variation 1 "Tooth Mechanic") | Rational/Value (Variation 4 "Benefits That Matter") |
| **Hypothesis** | The emotional hook will outperform on engagement and saves (aspirational content). The rational value stack will drive more actual applications (tangible differentiation). |
| **Success Metric** | Saves > 50 (emotional), Application completions > 15 (rational) |
| **Duration** | 14 days |

### Test 3: Hook Type -- Patient Acquisition

| Element | Variation A | Variation B |
|---------|------------|------------|
| **Hook** | Food/sensory (Variation 2 "Foods You Miss" Reel) | Emotional/family (Variation 3 "Grandkids" Feed) |
| **Hypothesis** | Food Reel will capture higher cold-audience reach (food content performs well algorithmically). Family/grandkid image will convert at a higher rate (emotional specificity drives action). |
| **Success Metric** | Reach > 15K (food Reel), Consultation bookings > 5/week (family image) |
| **Duration** | 14 days, matched budgets |

### Test 4: Authority vs. Emotion -- Patient Acquisition

| Element | Variation A | Variation B |
|---------|------------|------------|
| **Approach** | Authority (Variation 4 "Dr. Olson" Reel) | Barrier Removal (Variation 5 "Financing First" Stories) |
