## CAMPAIGN NOTES

### YouTube-Specific Best Practices
- **First 5 seconds are everything:** YouTube's skip button appears at 5 seconds. The hook must be delivered and compelling before 0:05. Use pattern interrupt (stat, question, visual shock) not brand introduction.
- **Do not front-load the brand:** SGA logo and brand name should appear at the END, not the beginning. Leading with "SGA Dental Partners presents..." guarantees a skip.
- **Captions are mandatory:** Add burned-in captions for all spoken content. YouTube auto-captions are unreliable and many viewers watch with sound off.
- **Companion banner:** For all skippable in-stream ads, include a companion banner (300x60px) that persists on the page even after the ad is skipped. This captures residual awareness.
- **End screen CTA:** Use YouTube's end screen feature for 60-second ads -- overlay a subscribe button, link to landing page, and link to partner testimonial playlist.

### Retargeting Integration
- Build a YouTube retargeting audience from: viewers who watched 50%+ of any SGA ad, viewers who visited the channel page, viewers who engaged with companion banners.
- Retarget this audience on LinkedIn (via audience sync) and Facebook (via custom audiences) with mid/bottom-funnel conversion ads.
- YouTube awareness feeds LinkedIn and Google Search conversion campaigns. The funnel is: YouTube (see the story) -- LinkedIn/Facebook (see the proof) -- Google Search (capture the intent).

### Measurement Framework
- **Primary metric:** View-through rate (VTR) -- percentage of viewers who watch past the skip button. Target: 25%+ for 15-sec, 20%+ for 30-sec, 15%+ for 60-sec.
- **Secondary metrics:** Click-through rate to landing page, brand search lift (increase in "SGA Dental" branded searches on Google after YouTube campaign launch), career page visits.
- **Tertiary metrics:** YouTube subscriber growth on Dr. Olson's channel (if organic integration is running simultaneously), assisted conversions attributed to YouTube touchpoints.
