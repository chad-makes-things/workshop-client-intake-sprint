# Google Ads Campaigns -- SGA Dental Partners

**Prepared:** March 2026
**Platform:** Google Ads (Search -- Responsive Search Ads)
**Audiences:** Practice Owners searching "sell dental practice" keywords, Patients searching "dental implants near me" keywords
**Campaign Objectives:** Lead Generation (B2B), Conversions (B2C consultations)
**Brand Voice:** Sage archetype -- earned authority, specific, no hype. Patient-facing: clear, trustworthy, action-oriented.

---

## Campaign Structure Overview

### Campaign 1: B2B Practice Acquisition (Search)
- **Objective:** Leads (form submissions on partnership landing page)
- **Budget:** $3,000-$5,000/month
- **Bid Strategy:** Maximize conversions (initial); Target CPA after 30 conversions
- **Target CPA:** $200-$500 per qualified inquiry
- **Network:** Search only (no Display, no Performance Max for initial phase)

### Campaign 2: B2C Implant Patient Acquisition (Search)
- **Objective:** Conversions (virtual consultation bookings, phone calls)
- **Budget:** $2,000-$4,000/month (distributed across MODIS practice locations)
- **Bid Strategy:** Maximize conversions; Target CPA after 50 conversions
- **Target CPA:** $75-$130 per consultation booking
- **Network:** Search only

## CAMPAIGN NOTES

### Bidding Strategy Progression
1. **Weeks 1-3:** Maximize Conversions (let Google learn which searches convert)
2. **Weeks 4-8:** Target CPA (set CPA based on actual conversion data from weeks 1-3)
3. **Weeks 9+:** Target ROAS or manual CPC for top-performing keywords (if data supports)

### Landing Page Requirements
- B2B: Dedicated partnership landing page with lead form, Thurston track record, partner testimonials, and Practice Partnership Assessment download. Must load in under 3 seconds.
- B2C: Location-specific implant landing pages with transparent pricing, virtual consultation booking widget, before/after gallery, patient video testimonials, and financing calculator. Must be mobile-first.

### Quality Score Optimization
- Ensure keyword-to-ad relevance: each ad group should have tightly themed keywords (10-15 max per group)
- Include primary keywords in at least 3 headlines per RSA
- Landing page content must match the ad's promise (ad-to-page alignment is critical)
- Target Quality Score of 7+ for all primary keywords

### Tracking Setup
- Google Ads conversion tracking on: lead form submissions, phone calls (30+ seconds), virtual consultation bookings
- Google Analytics 4 event tracking for micro-conversions: assessment downloads, testimonial page views, pricing page views
- Offline conversion import from CRM: track which search leads become actual partnership conversations or patient appointments

### Budget Pacing
- B2B: Budget will pace slowly due to low search volume for practice transition keywords. This is expected and acceptable -- each conversion is extremely high value.
- B2C: Budget will pace quickly for high-volume patient keywords. Use dayparting to focus spend during peak hours (8 AM - 12 PM, 6 PM - 10 PM) when patients are most likely to book.
