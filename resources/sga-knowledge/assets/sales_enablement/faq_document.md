## CATEGORY 1: PARTNERSHIP PROCESS

### Q1: How does the partnership process work? What are the steps?

**Underlying objection:** "Is this going to be a drawn-out, opaque process where I lose control?"

SGA follows a transparent, three-step partnership path designed to feel like a conversation between peers, not a corporate acquisition process. There are no brokers involved, no high-pressure timelines, and no hidden steps.

**Step 1: Explore.** You schedule a confidential conversation with our partnership team. This is a mutual discovery call -- we learn about your practice, your goals, and your concerns, and you learn about SGA's model, culture, and track record. We evaluate fit on both sides: financials matter, but so do culture, clinical philosophy, and vision. Many of the best partnerships start with a 45-minute call where the dentist was "just curious."

**Step 2: Partner.** If mutual interest exists, we walk through a structured evaluation. You receive an honest valuation based on transparent methodology. We structure a joint-venture equity partnership tailored to your practice profile -- community practice, group practice, or specialty practice. You retain an ownership stake in the combined SGA platform, and your upside does not end at closing.

**Step 3: Thrive.** Post-close, SGA transitions back-office operations (billing, payroll, HR, compliance, procurement, marketing, recruiting) within 60-90 days. You are paired with a clinical mentor through the Individual Growth Engine. You attend the 4Front Symposium and connect with 100+ peer clinicians. Your name stays. Your team stays. You get back to dentistry.

**Closer:** The typical timeline from first conversation to close is 3-6 months, depending on practice complexity. We do not rush partners. We also do not disappear for weeks at a time. You will have a direct point of contact throughout.

### Q2: How long does the partnership process take from start to finish?

**Underlying objection:** "I do not want this hanging over my head for a year."

We control what we can control and communicate clearly when external factors -- legal review, landlord consent, state-specific regulatory requirements -- add time.

**Closer:** We have closed partnerships in as few as 90 days and as many as 9 months. The pace is set by you, not us. What we commit to: you will never wonder where things stand. Your BD contact provides weekly updates during active evaluation.

### Q6: How does the equity model work? What does "retained equity" mean in practice?

**Underlying objection:** "The equity did not turn out to be what they thought" -- this is the #1 financial fear.

When you partner with SGA, you receive a meaningful liquidity event (typically 60-70% of the agreed valuation) in cash at close. The remaining 30-40% is "rolled" into equity in the combined SGA platform. This means you own shares in the entire SGA organization -- not just your individual practice, but the whole network of 100+ practices across multiple states and three verticals.

Your equity appreciates as the platform grows. Every new practice that joins, every operational efficiency gained, every revenue increase across the network increases the value of your stake. This is the "second bite of the apple" that makes the SGA model fundamentally different from an outright acquisition (where you get cash and walk away) or a management services arrangement (where you pay fees and get no platform upside).

The Thurston Group has executed this exact model across 8 dental and healthcare platforms and has returned over $4 billion to partners. The equity appreciation story is not theoretical. It has happened repeatedly, and our existing partners can speak to it directly.

**Closer:** We can connect you with current SGA partners who will tell you exactly how the equity model has performed for them. Not projections. Not marketing materials. Real people. Real numbers.
