This is not a document to skim and forget. Here is how practice owners who have used this framework get the most value:

1. **Block 15 minutes.** Close the door, silence the phone, and score yourself honestly. Aspirational answers help no one.

2. **Pay attention to Category 5.** Financial health and market position are important, but Personal Readiness (burnout, succession, emotional readiness) is where most owners discover something they have been avoiding.

3. **Use the DSO checklist in every conversation.** Whether you talk to SGA, a competitor, or a broker who cold-calls next Tuesday, the 22 questions in Section 7 will tell you more about a potential partner than any pitch deck.

## Page Design Notes

**Visual tone:** Consistent with the opt-in page. Clean, professional, warm. Confirmation checkmark icon at top. No clutter.

**Layout:** Single-column, generous white space. The three path options should be visually distinct cards (not buried in running text). Each card has an icon, a short description, and a clear CTA button.

**No pop-ups, no exit-intent overlays.** This page has already converted. The goal is trust-building and next-step selection, not pressure.

**Mobile:** Three path options stack vertically on mobile. Download backup button is prominent above the fold.

**Tracking:**
- Path 1 click = tag `pa_newsletter_interest`
- Path 2 click = tag `pa_peer_conversation_request` + alert to BD team
- Path 3 click = tag `pa_discovery_call_request` + high-priority alert to BD team
- Direct download click = tracked to measure email delivery issues
