## Page Design Notes

**Visual tone:** Clean, professional, warm but not flashy. Sage archetype -- think trusted advisor, not used-car salesman. Navy and white palette with warm accent color. Generous white space.

**Typography:** Serif headlines (authority, tradition) paired with clean sans-serif body text (readability, modernity).

**No countdown timers, no pop-ups, no "only X copies left" scarcity tactics.** This audience is sophisticated (Schwartz Level 4) and will see through manufactured urgency. The page earns trust through substance, not pressure.

**Mobile optimization:** Form fields large enough to tap easily. Assessment mockup visible above the fold on mobile. CTA button sticky on mobile scroll.

**Page speed:** Target < 2 seconds load time. No heavy video embeds on the landing page itself.
