## Email 2: Education -- The Three-Step Partnership Path

**Send:** Day 3

**Subject Line Options:**
1. How a partnership conversation actually works (step by step)
2. Connect. Engage. Partner. Three steps, zero surprises.
3. What happens when you pick up the phone (it is not what you think)

**Preview Text:** No brokers. No NDAs at this stage. Here is exactly what to expect.

---

**Body:**

Dr. {{first_name}},

One reason practice owners delay the partnership conversation is that they do not know what it looks like. And the unknown creates anxiety.

Will I be pressured? Will my financial information be shared? Will a broker start calling me three times a day?

Let me remove the ambiguity. Here is exactly how the SGA partnership process works:

**Step 1: Connect (Week 1)**

You schedule a 30-minute confidential conversation with our partnership team. This is not a sales call. It is a mutual exploration. You tell us about your practice -- your revenue, your team, your clinical focus, your vision. We tell you about SGA -- our structure, our verticals, our education platform.

At the end of the call, you decide whether to continue the conversation. If not, nothing changes. No follow-up pressure. No broker callbacks.

**Step 2: Engage (Weeks 2-6)**

If there is mutual interest, we walk through a more detailed evaluation. We share how the equity model works for a practice like yours. You share operational and financial data (under NDA at this stage) so we can build an honest valuation.

During this phase, we encourage you to talk directly with current SGA partners. Not our business development team. The actual dentists who made this decision and live with the outcome every day. We will give you their phone numbers.

**Step 3: Partner (Weeks 6-12+)**

If the fit is right for both sides, we present a formal partnership proposal. You review it with your accountant, your attorney, your financial advisor -- anyone you trust. There is no countdown clock. There is no "this offer expires Friday." A career-defining decision deserves time.

If you move forward, the transition begins: operational handoff, technology investment planning, mentor pairing, and a clear 90-day onboarding timeline for you and your team.

**What we do NOT do:**

- We do not share your information with brokers
- We do not cold-call you after you say "not right now"
- We do not pressure you with artificial deadlines
- We do not make promises we cannot back with a phone call to a current partner

This is a trust funnel, not a sales funnel. The timeline is yours.

Ready to start with Step 1?
